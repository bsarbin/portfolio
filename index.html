How I work

I design because I enjoy helping people. By simplifying workflows and making applications more approachable, I help users become superstars at work and at home. When I design, I'm always evaluating if I'm addressing the most critical problems for users and providing the simplest possible solutions. This mindset also helps me to drive consensus around ideas because I'm always looking out for the user and will champion the best idea no matter where it comes from. I also like to explain my design rationale while working with development teams, including pointing out the weaknesses in my designs. I've found this practice helps solve some of the tougher challenges and it creates a strong sense of trust on the team. I've also seen their work become more user-centered, improving the overall product.
